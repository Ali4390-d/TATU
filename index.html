Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Innovation

TATU brings a modern twist to traditional Chinese cuisine. Founded by award-winning Chef Liang, our restaurants blend authentic flavors with contemporary culinary techniques.

From our signature Peking Duck to our artisanal dim sum, every dish is crafted with precision and passion. We source the finest ingredients to create an unforgettable dining experience that honors Chinese heritage while embracing modern aesthetics.
